## [2026-05] Brand Foundation — two-tier architecture

**What**: Quick Reference restructured into Overarching and Market-Localized
sub-sections. Foundation Lab gained a tier selector at the top.
"Internal" audience renamed to "Employer Branding" throughout.

**Why**: Clearer mental model separating universal brand positioning
(locked, Brand overarching) from audience- and market-specific adaptations
generated by the Lab.

**Affects**: `foundation-data.ts`, `QuickReference.tsx`, `FoundationLab.tsx`,
`page.tsx`, `brand-foundation.module.css`.

---

## [2026-05] Foundation Lab — source always locked to Brand overarching

**What**: Removed source dimension picker from Foundation Lab.
Source is always Brand overarching v2026.

**Why**: All variants derive from one canonical source. Allowing arbitrary
source selection risked "drift chains" where a variant was generated from
another variant rather than the canonical truth.

**Affects**: `FoundationLab.tsx`, `app/api/foundation-lab/route.ts`.
